South East Midlands Local Enterprise Partnership

A £39 billion economy, home to the British F1 Grand Prix

Advanced Manufacturing

Aerospace

The region has a well-established aerospace and defence & electronics cluster, including companies such as Safran Power, BE Aerospace, Selex, Airbus and Rolls Royce as well as MROs at London Luton Airport such as Gulfstream Aerospace Ltd, the first service centre outside of the USA. Investors can also benefit from access to over £2 billion of national funding to support the next generation of aircraft, managed by the Aerospace Technology Institute (ATI) at Cranfield University).

Rail

The South East Midlands is home to Network Rail’s National Centre and is at the heart of Britain’s railway operations. Daventry International Rail Freight Terminal (DIRFT), a railroad intermodal freight terminal with an associated warehousing estate, is a leading distribution location, complemented by the Eurohub freight terminal at Corby.

Food & Drink manufacturing and processing

With good connectivity to 80% of the UK’s population, the area is home to several significant food and drink manufacturing and distribution centres including Carlsberg, AG Barr, Barry Callebaut, Brioche Pasquier, Arla Dairies, Coca-Cola, Weetabix and McCormick (Schwartz).
